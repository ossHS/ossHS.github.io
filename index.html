What is ossHS?

ossHS is a 48-hour hackathon where high school students can build cool projects for the front-end, backend, or for native desktop and mobile cliets, except there's a catch: it has to be open-source.

Where is ossHS?

In short, anywhere! It's completely online and virtual, so that means you can sit at home and hack some cool stuff up, all while being part of a global competition.

Where can I talk to other participants?

To discuss ossHS and chat with others during the competition, you can head over to our subreddit at /r/ossHS or on our IRC channel at #ossHS.
